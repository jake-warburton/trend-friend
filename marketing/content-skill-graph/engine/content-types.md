# Recurring Content Types & Series

Formats that build habit and audience when repeated consistently.

## Weekly Trend Drop

**Format**: ranked list of the week's biggest movers with scores
**Cadence**: every Friday
**Platforms**: X thread + LinkedIn + IG carousel
**Template**: "this week's top 5 breakout trends (scored across 28 sources): 1. [trend] — jumped from #X to #Y..."
**Why it works**: people come back for the list. becomes appointment content

## "Nobody's Talking About This"

**Format**: single contrarian callout of an emerging trend that hasn't hit mainstream yet
**Cadence**: 1-2x/week
**Platforms**: X + TikTok
**Template**: "[topic] just hit rank #X on Signal Eye. [number] sources picked it up. zero mainstream coverage yet. here's what's happening..."
**Why it works**: makes the audience feel like insiders. drives FOMO

## Source Showdown

**Format**: compare what different source types are saying about the same topic
**Cadence**: 1x/week
**Platforms**: X + IG carousel
**Template**: "Reddit says X is huge. GitHub says it's dying. Wikipedia just noticed it. here's what the full picture looks like when you score all 28..."
**Why it works**: shows the unique value of multi-source scoring

## Build in Public

**Format**: product update, technical decision, or behind-the-scenes moment
**Cadence**: 1-2x/week
**Platforms**: X + TikTok
**Template**: "just shipped [feature]. here's why..." or "28 sources. 45 minute refresh. here's what the pipeline looks like"
**Why it works**: builds trust, attracts builder audience, shows momentum

## Trend Autopsy

**Format**: deep dive on a trend that peaked and faded — what the data showed
**Cadence**: 2x/month
**Platforms**: LinkedIn + Blog
**Template**: "remember when everyone was talking about [topic]? here's exactly when it peaked, which sources called it first, and what happened after"
**Why it works**: proves the scoring engine works retrospectively. very shareable

## Dashboard Walkthroughs

**Format**: screen recording scrolling through Signal Eye with narration
**Cadence**: 1x/week
**Platforms**: TikTok + IG reels
**Template**: quick 30-45s tour of an interesting corner of the data
**Why it works**: product demo disguised as content. shows the actual UI
