# Hook Formulas

Scroll-stopping openers organised by type. Match to [[platform-tone]] before using.

## Data Hook

Lead with a specific, surprising number from Signal Eye data.

- "X just jumped 14 ranks in 48 hours. here's what the data says"
- "we track 28 sources. only 3 of them saw this coming"
- "this topic scored higher than AI this week. nobody's talking about it"
- "[topic] went from rank 187 to rank 12 in one week"

Best on: X, LinkedIn, TikTok

## Contrarian Hook

Challenge what people assume is trending (or not).

- "everyone's talking about X. the data says Y is the real story"
- "this trend is quietly dying and nobody noticed"
- "the most overhyped topic this week according to 28 data sources"
- "stop watching [obvious thing]. here's what's actually emerging"

Best on: X, LinkedIn

## Behind-the-Build Hook

Show the product being built. Builder audience loves this.

- "we just added [feature]. here's why it changes everything about how we score trends"
- "28 sources ingested every 45 minutes. here's what that looks like"
- "built a trend scoring engine. it found something I didn't expect"

Best on: X, TikTok, Instagram stories

## Question Hook

Open with a question that the data answers.

- "what if you could see what's emerging before it trends?"
- "how do you know if a trend is real or just noise?"
- "what do Reddit, GitHub, Google Trends, and Wikipedia have in common this week?"

Best on: LinkedIn, Instagram carousels

## List/Ranking Hook

People love ranked lists. We literally produce them.

- "top 5 breakout trends this week (and why they matter)"
- "the 3 topics that scored highest across all 28 sources"
- "this week's fastest risers — ranked by our scoring engine"

Best on: all platforms

## Show-Don't-Tell Hook (Visual)

Screenshot or screen recording of the actual dashboard.

- [screenshot of trend jumping ranks] + "this happened overnight"
- [screen recording scrolling the explorer] + "28 sources. one ranking. updated every 45 minutes"
- [score breakdown chart] + "here's why this trend scored higher than everything else"

Best on: TikTok, Instagram, X (with image)
