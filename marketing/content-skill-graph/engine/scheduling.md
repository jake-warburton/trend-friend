# Scheduling & Cadence

## Weekly Posting Targets

| Platform | Posts/week | Best times (UTC) |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| X | 5-7 | 13:00-15:00 (US morning), 08:00-09:00 (UK morning) |
| LinkedIn | 3 | Tue/Wed/Thu 08:00-09:00 UTC |
| Instagram | 3 | Mon/Wed/Fri, evening |
| TikTok | 3-5 | 17:00-20:00 UTC |
| Blog | 1-2 | Tue or Thu morning |

## Content Mix (weekly)

- 2x **data drops** — breakout trends, weekly rankings, surprising movers
- 2x **insight posts** — "here's what this trend means for X"
- 1x **behind-the-build** — product updates, how Signal Eye works, building in public
- 1x **engagement/question** — ask audience what they're seeing, polls
- 1x **repurposed thread/carousel** — take best performing X post and expand

## Data-Driven Posting

Signal Eye runs every 45 minutes. Use the freshest data:

- **Monday**: weekend movers recap — what changed while people were offline
- **Wednesday**: mid-week breakout spotlight — the trend with the biggest jump
- **Friday**: weekly rankings roundup — top 5 with scores and sources
- **Ad hoc**: any time a trend jumps 10+ ranks in < 24 hours, post immediately
