# X / Twitter

Primary growth channel. Builder-twitter audience.

## Format Rules

- Single tweets: max 280 chars. one idea. one hook
- Threads: 3-7 tweets. each tweet stands alone. number them
- Images: dashboard screenshots perform well. always include alt text
- No hashtags unless genuinely relevant (#buildinpublic is fine, #trending is not)

## Style

- Lowercase preferred. casual energy
- One thought per line. use line breaks
- Lead with the number or the surprise
- End tweets with a soft CTA or open question — not "like and retweet"
- Apply [[brand-voice]] at its most casual

## What Works

- Specific data points from Signal Eye ("rank #47 -> #3 in 48 hours")
- Contrarian takes backed by data
- Build-in-public updates
- Screenshots of the dashboard showing something interesting
- Threads breaking down a single trend with sources

## What Doesn't Work

- Generic "AI is the future" takes without data
- Reposting news without adding Signal Eye's angle
- Too many links (kills reach)
- Corporate tone

## Audience Here

Primarily [[founders-pms]] and [[creators]]. Some [[investors]].
