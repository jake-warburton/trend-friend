# LinkedIn

Professional narrative. Thought leadership backed by data.

## Format Rules

- 800-1500 words. whitespace matters — break up paragraphs
- Use bullet points for data, flowing prose for narrative
- First 2 lines must hook (they show before "see more")
- End with a question to drive comments
- 1-3 relevant hashtags max at the bottom

## Style

- Still casual builder tone, but slightly more professional than X
- Personal angle: "I noticed something in this week's data..."
- Tell a story around the numbers. context > raw data
- Apply [[brand-voice]] with [[platform-tone]] LinkedIn adjustments

## What Works

- "Here's what our trend engine found this week" with a personal narrative
- Contrarian insights: "everyone thinks X is hot, the data says otherwise"
- Trend autopsies — retrospective analysis of peaks and fades
- Industry implications of emerging trends

## What Doesn't Work

- Walls of unbroken text
- Pure self-promotion without insight
- Repasting an X thread without adaptation
- Buzzword-heavy corporate speak

## Audience Here

Primarily [[founders-pms]], [[seo-teams]], and [[investors]].
