# Blog (Post-MVP)

Long-form SEO content. The evergreen growth channel.

## Format Rules

- 1000-2500 words
- SEO-optimised: target long-tail keywords around trend topics
- Structure: hook -> context -> Signal Eye data -> analysis -> implications -> CTA
- Include 2-4 embedded dashboard screenshots with alt text
- Internal links to related trend pages on Signal Eye

## Target Keywords Pattern

- "[topic] trends 2026"
- "emerging [topic] trends"
- "is [topic] growing"
- "[topic] market signals"
- "what's trending in [industry]"

## Style

- Most thorough version of [[brand-voice]]
- Data-heavy but readable. explain the scoring methodology briefly
- Link to the actual Signal Eye explorer pages for each trend mentioned
- Include a "methodology" note: "Signal Eye aggregates 28+ sources..."

## Audience Here

Primarily [[seo-teams]] who find it via search and share internally.
Secondarily [[founders-pms]] and [[investors]] who want deep analysis.

## Repurposing

Blog posts get sliced back into X threads, LinkedIn posts, and IG carousels.
Reverse of the normal chain — long-form becomes short-form.
