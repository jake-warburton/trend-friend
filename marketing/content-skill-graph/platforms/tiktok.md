# TikTok

Raw, unfiltered trend breakdowns. Screen recordings of the actual product.

## Format Rules

- 30-60 seconds. hook in first 2 seconds
- Screen recording of Signal Eye dashboard with voiceover narration
- No heavy editing. raw > polished. authenticity wins
- Captions/subtitles always on (most watch muted)
- Vertical format only

## Script Structure

1. **Hook** (0-2s): "okay so [surprising finding]"
2. **Show** (2-15s): navigate to the trend in the dashboard, point at the score
3. **Explain** (15-40s): what the sources say, why it matters
4. **CTA** (40-45s): "follow for more trend data" or "comment what you want me to look up"

## Style

- Most casual of all platforms. talk like you're on a call with a friend
- Show genuine surprise/curiosity when the data is interesting
- Screen recording > talking head (but mix it up)
- Apply [[brand-voice]] at maximum casual

## What Works

- "I track 28 data sources. here's what they're all saying right now"
- Reacting to a trend that just broke out in the rankings
- "Which is more hyped: [X] vs [Y]? let's check the data"
- Quick dashboard tours showing interesting patterns

## What Doesn't Work

- Over-produced content
- Reading from a script (conversational > rehearsed)
- Content longer than 60s unless genuinely riveting
- Being too salesy about the product

## Audience Here

Broader reach. Mix of [[creators]], younger [[founders-pms]], and curious generalists.
