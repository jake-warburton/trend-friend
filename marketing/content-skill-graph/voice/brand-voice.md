# Brand Voice — Signal Eye

## Identity

Signal Eye is a trend intelligence terminal. Not a dashboard. Not an analytics tool. A terminal — like a Bloomberg terminal for trends.

We track what's emerging across 28+ sources before it hits the mainstream. We score it. We rank it. We tell you why it matters.

## Tone

- **Casual builder-twitter style.** Write like you're telling a smart friend what you found interesting today
- **Data-backed, not hype.** Always lead with specific numbers, rankings, scores. "AI agents jumped 14 ranks in 48 hours" not "AI agents are trending"
- **Opinionated but earned.** We have data to back up takes. State the insight, show the signal
- **Concise over clever.** Short sentences. No filler words. Get to the point
- **Lowercase energy.** Don't shout. Confidence is quiet. Use caps sparingly

## Language Rules

- Say "signal" not "data point"
- Say "surface" not "find" or "discover"
- Say "emerging" not "trending" (everyone says trending)
- Say "scored" or "ranked" not "went viral"
- Say "sources" not "platforms we monitor"
- Say "breakout" for fast risers, "steady" for consistent, "fading" for declining
- Never say "game-changer", "revolutionary", "cutting-edge", "leverage"
- Never use corporate buzzwords. Write like a human

## What We Are

- An intelligence layer, not a news aggregator
- A scoring engine, not a popularity contest
- A signal detector, not a trend follower
- Built for people who act on trends, not just observe them

## What We're Not

- Not another Google Trends wrapper
- Not social listening (we go way beyond social)
- Not a dashboard you check once and forget
- Not enterprise-only — individual builders use it too

## Personality Traits

- Quietly confident — the data speaks
- Curious — genuinely interested in what's emerging
- Builder-minded — we use our own tool to build our own product
- Contrarian-friendly — sometimes the interesting signal is what ISN'T trending
