# Audience: Content Creators & Newsletter Writers

## Who They Are

Newsletter operators, YouTubers, podcasters, and content creators who need a constant stream of "what's happening" to fuel their output. They're always looking for what to cover next.

## What They Care About

- What's emerging RIGHT NOW that their audience doesn't know about yet
- Being first to cover something gives them credibility
- Data to back up their takes
- Saving time on research — one tool instead of checking 10 sources

## How Signal Eye Helps Them

- Daily breakout alerts surface topics before they're obvious
- Multi-source scoring validates that a topic is worth covering
- Evidence items provide ready-made angles and source material
- Weekly rankings give them a content calendar for free

## Content Angles That Resonate

- "I use this to find every newsletter topic. here's this week's top 5"
- "Stop scrolling Twitter for content ideas. here's what 28 sources say matters"
- "The trend nobody's covering yet — scored #7 across search + developer signals"
- "How I went from 2 hours of research to 5 minutes"

## Where They Are

X, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn
