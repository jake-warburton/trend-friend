# Audience: Founders & Product Managers

## Who They Are

Startup founders and product managers at growth-stage companies. They're making bets on what to build next and need to know what's emerging before competitors do.

## What They Care About

- Market timing — is this trend real enough to build on?
- Competitive intelligence — what are others building toward?
- Opportunity signals — where's the whitespace?
- Validation — is this idea already gaining traction elsewhere?

## How Signal Eye Helps Them

- Multi-source scoring proves a trend isn't just social media noise
- Cross-platform corroboration shows genuine market interest
- Historical tracking shows if a trend is accelerating or fading
- Ad intelligence (Pro) shows which trends have commercial validation

## Content Angles That Resonate

- "X is emerging across developer, social, AND search signals — that's rare"
- "This trend scored low on social but high on developer. Classic early-stage signal"
- "3 trends that look like noise but score high on commercial validation"
- Tool comparisons: how Signal Eye differs from Google Trends, Exploding Topics, etc

## Where They Are

X (builder-twitter), LinkedIn, sometimes TikTok
