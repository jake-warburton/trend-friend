# Audience: Investors & Analysts

## Who They Are

VCs, angel investors, and market analysts tracking emerging technology and market signals. They need early indicators of what's gaining traction to inform investment theses.

## What They Care About

- Leading indicators — signals before mainstream awareness
- Cross-source validation — is this trend real across multiple ecosystems?
- Momentum — is this accelerating, steady, or fading?
- Commercial validation — are advertisers spending on this? (Ad Intelligence)

## How Signal Eye Helps Them

- 5-dimension scoring separates hype from substance
- Developer signals (GitHub, npm, PyPI) show builder adoption
- Search signals show genuine market demand
- Advertising signals show commercial interest
- Historical tracking shows trajectory, not just a snapshot

## Content Angles That Resonate

- "Developer activity on [topic] is up 300% but mainstream attention is flat. Classic pre-breakout signal"
- "Where VCs should be looking: 5 trends with high developer + search scores and zero media coverage"
- "This trend's advertising score just spiked — companies are putting money behind it"
- "Signal Eye vs. reading 15 newsletters every morning"

## Where They Are

LinkedIn primarily. Some X (fintwit overlap).
