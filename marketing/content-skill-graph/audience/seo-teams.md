# Audience: SEO & Growth Teams

## Who They Are

SEO managers, growth marketers, and content strategists at organisations. They track search trends professionally and need multi-source intelligence to inform content and keyword strategies.

## What They Care About

- Search volume trends — what's gaining search interest?
- Content opportunities — what should they write about next?
- Competitive gaps — what trends are competitors missing?
- Commercial intent — are advertisers bidding on these keywords? (Ad Intelligence)
- Internal reporting — data they can share with leadership to justify strategy

## How Signal Eye Helps Them

- Search signal scoring validated against 27 other source types
- Ad intelligence shows CPC and advertiser activity alongside organic trends
- Cross-source corroboration proves a trend is real, not just a search spike
- Weekly exports and API access for integration into existing workflows
- Score breakdowns they can drop into strategy decks

## Content Angles That Resonate

- "Google Trends says X is flat. But developer + social signals are surging. Here's the SEO play"
- "5 keywords where search volume is about to spike (based on leading indicators)"
- "How to spot content opportunities 2-4 weeks before your competitors"
- "Stop using Google Trends alone. Here's what you're missing"
- "Ad Intelligence: see which keywords competitors are bidding on before you write"

## Where They Are

LinkedIn, blog/SEO content (they find us via search), X occasionally.

## Conversion Path

Most likely to convert to Pro — they have budget and the ad intelligence dashboard is directly actionable for their work.
